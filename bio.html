Ross joined the Army straight out of high school, serving with the 75th Ranger Regiment on three combat deployments overseas. Between deployments he applied and was accepted to West Point, joining the Class of 2007. At West Point he was the captain of the Combat Marksmanship Team and a member of the Brigade Respect Council. He graduated with a B.S. in History and a concentration in Computer Science. After graduating from West Point Ross joined the 1st Cavalry Division, where he spent five years, to include another deployment to Baghdad. While deployed he was tasked to create, equip, and train an elite Iraqi Commando unit which partnered in operations with with U.S. special operations forces such as the 75th Rangers and Navy SEALs.
